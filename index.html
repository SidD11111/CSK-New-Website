About Us

After completing the course rigor of high school Computer Science, we realized how much we would have benefited from exposure to the subject in previous years and decided to start a non-profit to do just that. Held at Conant High School, CompSci Kids allows elementary and junior high students to have a background in computer science before high school. The hope is to cultivate within these students a passion for learning and logical thought that will greatly benefit them in the future. Through the use of online programs, fun activities, and exciting projects, CompSci Kids provides an enticing venue for students to learn about computer science!
